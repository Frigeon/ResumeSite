Programming Related

GitHub:
Git is a wonderful site if you want to work on almost any sort of coding as I am discovering more and more. If you get a chance look up Frigeon. Be warned though, so far I have many incomplete projects.
Project Euler:
An excellent resource if you just want to get to know the basics of a new language, or if you want to challenge your mind to some of the math puzzles on this phenomenal site.
XNA Game Studio:
A good site to visit and check out some of the resources if you think you might want to get involved in game programming.
DropBox:
An excellent cloud-based backup system with free accounts up to 2GB.
Random Dungeon Generator:
Quite an article about random dungeon generation, useful for games, or for those of us who play Dungeons and Dragons.

Green Housing Related

Earthship Biotecture:
One of the most interesting concepts for green housing and off-grid living, I helped build one during the summer of 2010 and got to meed the whole crew. Some great people and great ideas!
Middle Earth Home:
A couple of great people I met while building the earthship for my parents, fairly locally situated and a real blast to chat with!

Chain and Metalwork Related

The Maille Artisans International League:
Just about anything and everything you may or may not have wanted to know about chainmail and chain jewelery. Also a forum filled with interesting people!
ChainMail Basket:
The interesting site that got me started working on chainmail and one of the first I found when I decided to look into doing it.
AnvilFire:
A great deal of information about blacksmithing and knowledgeable people can be found here.
ABANA:
The Artist Blacksmith Association of North America, some of the most dedicated people involved in metalwork today almost every one of these groups is working to keep blacksmithing alive today.
